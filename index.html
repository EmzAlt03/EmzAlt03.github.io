Expectations at RMIT

The Bachelor’s Degree in Information Technology lasts approximately four years or 16 semesters total. Review of the RMIT homepage highlights a basic course structure with 32 units to be completed, some of which are minor subjects. The degree is built on the credit point system with each course delivering 12 credit points for a passing grade. The subjects I’m most interested in revolving around cyber security, system maintenance and database design. The course covers a variety of fields including programming, cloud computing, user authentication, user centered design etc. The difficulty of understanding concepts varies between subject with some more practical than others. Overall, my personal expectations for the course are of a high standard though, hard work and dedication to my studies including assessments will most definitely resolve many personal doubts along the way
